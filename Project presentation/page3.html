The Big Dipper

* When we first interacted with the artwork, we really liked its idea of stars and constellations.

* So we chose a specific constellation to present the artwork, which is the Big Dipper.

* We tried to mimic the actual "starry night" on our site.

* We like the way that each star opens a different email message, so our stars in the Big Dipper direct to different pages of our presentation.
